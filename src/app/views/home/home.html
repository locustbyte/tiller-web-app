Start building your Tiller portfolio

There are three different types of Tiller portfolio to choose from, each giving you a different level of control over how your money’s invested.

1

Tracker

Invest in Exchange Traded Funds (ETFs) that match the performance of a selected ‘index’ such as the FTSE 100.

2

Active

Invest in a portfolio of ETFs with a selection of hand-picked ‘active’ funds which aim to outperform an ‘index’ such as the FTSE 100.

3

Active Plus

For even greater control of your investment you can add one or more of five investment fund ‘themes’ into the asset mix.
